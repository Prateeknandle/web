DESIGN QUEST

ABOUT

It is a UI/UX based design competition encouraging the young designers to exhibit their designing skills and build an attractive interface of an application.

Procedure

1. Each member of a team needs to register separately through the Techotsav website.
2. This will generate a unique Cogni ID for each member.
3. Then the team leader (which you will select among yourself) needs to log in on the website through his username and password and register for the event by entering details of all the team members.
4. This will generate your Team ID and a mail shall be sent to the Email ID of the team leader about the confirmation of the registration.
5. For any query, go to contact us section.
