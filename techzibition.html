TECHZIBITION

About

Present your science innovation ideas at this stupendous exhibition in front of 20k+ students, experienced professors, renowned speakers, and eminent personalities.

Rules

1. Student pursuing Undergraduate/Master’s Degree in any discipline is eligible for this event.
2. You can submit your abstract on the Techotsav website.
3. To submit the Abstract go to your dashboard, select the event and upload it in pdf format.
4. The deadline for the submission of abstracts is 10th March 2020.
5. There will be four categories for evaluation of the Project/Model
○ Design
○ Development
○ Robotics
○ Miscellaneous
6. The Top 15 abstracts of each category will be shortlisted upon evaluation to exhibit at TECHZIBITION IITT GWALIOR.
7. The shortlisted participants will be notified personally through email.
8. Each team can have maximum five members.

Procedure

1. Each member of a team needs to register separately through the Techotsav website.
2. This will generate a unique registration ID for each member.
3. Then the team leader (which you will select among yourself) needs to log in on the website through his username and password and register for the event by entering details of all the team members.
4. This will generate your Team ID and a mail shall be sent to the Email ID of the team leader about the confirmation of the registration.
5. For any query, go to contact us section.
